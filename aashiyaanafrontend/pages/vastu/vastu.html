Tips from Vastu Shastra

The main door would have to be in the northeast or east preferably, otherwise in the north or west.
The south-facing entrance is to be avoided.

The kitchen should be in a south east direction.
The east-facing cooking platform should be given preference.

Keep water tanker, sinks, taps, etc. in the north east direction.

Master Bedroom (Couple Bedroom) should be in a South West direction.
The bed should be placed certain that your head should be in East or South direction while you sleep.

Always place the wardrobe (almirah) in the Northeast and Southwest corner.
Wardrobe shouldn't face your bed.

Mirrors must be placed on the east or north side walls of the home.

Nameplate should be good blend and burst of colors
Your names should be written in red/shades of red
because the color symoblizes prosperity,fame and vigour.

The northern wall and northern areas of your home should preferably be in
shades of green(light green, pistachio green) or violet.
Any other home decor or show pieces in the same direction should also be in
green color and hues as the color brings harmony and healing.

You can put real plants and florets on north and north-western sides.

For the east zones shades of yellow and orange with a mix of marron shades are beneficial
as they symbolise prosperity and wealth.

West zones can have orange shades and if any frames/pictures are put in that direction
then avoid having pictures that have a message of negativity/end of something
like setting sun, submerging ships,etc in lieu one can have frames of something that indicates a rise of positivity
like a flowing river or beginning of a journey.

South directed areas can have red and blue from the color palette.
Also a wooden and blue color combo is a good match as blue brings with it health,peace and profoundness in thoughts.

Keep natural plants and flowers in place of artificial ones because they help increase oxygen levels and soothes your eyes.

If you plan to have a money plant do not keep it inside the house, plant it out of the house or in the balcony out.
